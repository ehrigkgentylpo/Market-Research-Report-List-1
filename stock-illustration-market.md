<p><p>Stock illustration markets are categorized into three main types: macrostock, midstock, and microstock. Macrostock consists of high-quality, exclusive imagery often sold at premium prices, intended for commercial purposes. Midstock features good-quality images at medium price points, catering to diverse client needs. Microstock, on the other hand, offers affordable, royalty-free images with a focus on volume sales, making it accessible for smaller budgets and casual users. Each market serves different segments of clients based on quality and price sensitivity.</p></p>
