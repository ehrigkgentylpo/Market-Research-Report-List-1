<p><p>The Liver Function Tests market is segmented into various types that assess liver health. The Alanine Aminotransferase (ALT) Test measures liver enzyme levels, indicating liver damage. The Aspartate Aminotransferase (AST) Test evaluates similar enzyme activity. The Alkaline Phosphatase Test checks for bile duct obstruction. The Gamma GT Test measures another enzyme linked to liver disease. Total Bilirubin assesses the liver's ability to process waste, while Albumin evaluates protein production. Other tests may include imaging or specialized biomarkers for comprehensive liver assessment.</p></p>
