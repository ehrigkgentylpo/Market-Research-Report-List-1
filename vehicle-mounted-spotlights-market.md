<p><p>Vehicle mounted spotlights come in various types, including HID, incandescent, LED, and halogen lamps. HID (High-Intensity Discharge) lamps offer bright illumination and energy efficiency. Incandescent lights provide a warm glow but consume more power and have a shorter lifespan. LED spotlights are highly efficient, durable, and have a long lifespan, making them increasingly popular. Halogen lamps offer brighter light than traditional incandescent bulbs and have a better lifespan, but they can generate more heat. Each type serves different needs in the market.</p></p>
